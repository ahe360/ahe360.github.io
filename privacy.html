Privacy Policy for Autosort Extension

Autosort is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension, Autosort.

Information We Collect

Autosort does not collect, store, or share any personal information, browsing history, or download data. The extension only accesses the download paths to sort files into user-defined folders.

Use of Your Information

Since Autosort does not collect any personal data, we do not use or share any information.

Permissions

The following permissions are requested solely to enable the functionality of the extension:

  • Downloads: To monitor and manage download events for sorting files.
  • Storage: To store the paths of the designated folders for sorting downloaded files.

Third-Party Services

Autosort does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
